Casus — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 15 June 2026
Before you publish: replace [Operator name] and [contact email] below with your real details (a dedicated address such as support@casushealth.com is best).

1. Who we are

Casus is a clinical logbook that lets healthcare workers record, follow up and selectively share their clinical cases. Casus is operated by [Operator name]. For any privacy question, contact us at [contact email].

2. Information we collect

Account information — your name, email address, password (handled and protected by our authentication provider; we never see it in readable form), and the profile details you choose to add (training status, registration number, institution, specialty, year, country, your contact and a profile photo).

Case information you enter — the clinical content you record: title, diagnosis, specialty and sub-classification, level of involvement, supervisor, description, management, challenges, discussion, follow-up notes, and any photos, radiology images or investigation files you upload.

Patient information you choose to enter — optionally, a patient's name and contact number. You provide this at your own discretion for your own follow-up. It is visible only to you, the treating doctor; it is never included in any share link and is not shown to administrators.

3. How we use information

We use your information only to provide and operate the service: to sign you in, to store and display your logbook, to let you generate lists and case series, to let you share read-only links when you choose, and to keep the service secure. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising.

4. How information is stored and protected

Casus uses Supabase for its database, authentication and file storage, and Netlify for hosting. Information is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Access is controlled by database security rules so that each user can access only their own cases. Files (images and videos) are stored in the service's storage system.

5. Sharing and disclosure

Private by default. Your cases are visible only to you unless you act to share them.

Share links. If you create a share link, anyone holding that link can view that logbook's clinical cases on a read-only basis and may leave assessment marks. Patient names and contacts are excluded from shared views.

Administrators. A platform administrator can view clinical case content for oversight and quality purposes, but cannot see patient names or contacts.

Service providers. Supabase and Netlify process data on our behalf to run the service. We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

6. Your responsibilities

If you choose to enter patient-identifiable information, you are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful basis and any necessary consent under the laws that apply to you, including Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019. Only record patient information you are authorised to hold, and only what is necessary.

7. Data retention and deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. Deleting a case removes its content and associated files. You may request deletion of your account and its data by contacting [contact email].

8. Cookies and local storage

We store a sign-in token on your device so you stay logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children

Casus is intended for healthcare workers and is not directed to children.

10. International storage

Your information may be stored on servers located outside your country.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date above.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: [contact email].
